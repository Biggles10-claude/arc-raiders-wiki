Essential Tips & Tricks

Hard-earned wisdom from the Rust Belt

Beginner Tips (First 10 Hours)

1

Use Free Loadouts Aggressively

Zero risk way to learn. Scout maps, find POIs, complete low-risk quests, learn ARC spawns. Then trade the augment to Lance for an upgrade. Repeat until comfortable.

2

Recycle Everything (CRITICAL)

Never sell: Toasters, Desk Fans, Broken Radios, Damaged Electronics

Right-Click + Recycle:

  • Toaster = Plastic Parts + Wires
  • Fan = Metal Parts + Wires
  • Radio = Metal Parts + Electronics

Materials are the REAL currency.

3

Sound is Intelligence

  • Footsteps reveal player positions
  • Gunfire shows ongoing fights
  • Car alarms broadcast careless players
  • ARC have unique audio cues

Use headphones. Enable "3D Audio" in settings.

4

Blueprint Priority Protocol

Blueprint found = mission changes.

  1. STOP EVERYTHING
  2. Open inventory immediately
  3. Place in Safe Pocket
  4. Head to nearest extraction
  5. Ignore other loot, avoid all fights

Permanent unlock > any material

5

Heavy Ammo for ARC

  • Light ammo = pea shooter vs armored ARC
  • Medium ammo = okay vs ARC
  • Heavy ammo = ARC killer

Always bring a Ferro or Anvil for Bastions and Rocketeers.

Combat Tips

6

Melee Finish Downed Players

  • Bullets = 5-10 seconds + loud
  • Knockout Punch = 1 second + silent

Saves ammo, quieter, faster. Always melee downed enemies.

7

~100 HP Standard

Most Raiders have roughly 100 HP. Helps with engagement decisions.

Example: Ferro (40 dmg) = 3 shots to kill

Factor in shields. Headshots deal bonus damage.

8

Camera Melee Trick

Security cameras can be destroyed with jump + vertical crowbar swing.

Disables without ammo, silent, doesn't alert. Useful for stealth breaching.

9

Drone Propeller Focus

Wasps/Hornets have exposed propellers. Shoot same-side propeller = immediate crash.

Then melee to finish. Black smoke marks crash site. Quick kills = less attention.

10

Pre-Destroyed ARC Lootable

Dead ARC units ("husks") have loot. Don't overlook "cold" wrecks.

Giant spider-type = lootable hatch on top. Climb up, grab loot, jump off FAST (chassis heats up).

Movement & Survival

11

Stamina Management

Everything drains stamina: Sprinting, Jumping, Vaulting, Rolling

Slide while running = passive regen

Invest in Mobility skills FIRST. Running out = death sentence.

12

Plan Your Exit

Before dropping, know extraction points.

"I'll extract at X. Backup: if X is closed, go to Y."

Full bag = time to leave. Don't get greedy.

13

Parkour = Life

  • Vertical movement wins fights
  • Unexpected angles = advantage
  • Zipline + Snap Hook = mobility king
  • High ground = better sightlines
14

Car Alarm Hell

Vehicles with lights on = armed. Bump them = LOUD ALARM

Fix: Smash the attached alarm device FAST

Or just avoid lit vehicles entirely.

Looting & Efficiency

15

Watchlist Feature

Need specific materials?

  1. Hover over item in crafting
  2. Click eye icon
  3. Materials auto-highlight when looting

Saves time, prevents missing needed items. Essential for quests.

16

In-Round Crafting (Skill)

36-point Survival skill - GAME CHANGER

Craft during raids: Bandages, Shield Cells, Impact Grenades

Extends raid time indefinitely. Solo player essential. Team support amazing.

17

Flare Reading

Flare on ground = someone died there

BUT: Crafted flares can be decoys

Don't blindly charge flares. Could be a trap. Scan area first.

18

Locked Room = Best Loot

Breachable doors = high-value loot

  • Higher blueprint drop rates
  • Better container rolls
  • Worth the noise usually

Prioritize security rooms.

Advanced Strategy

19

Noise Discipline

  • Breaching = loud (use Gentle Pressure skill)
  • Prolonged fighting = attracts more
  • Finish fast or move on
  • Silent kills when possible (melee, suppressed)
20

Risk vs Reward Always

  • Got good loot? Consider extracting
  • Empty bag? Push further
  • Low HP? Play safe
  • High HP + resources? Be bold

Bank smaller gains > lose everything

21

Team Communication

Proximity chat is powerful.

  • "Don't shoot!" often works
  • Many players are friendly
  • Temporary alliances common
  • Take down ARC together

Then extract peacefully... or betray at extraction (your choice).

22

Free Loadout Strategies

  • Loot Goblin: Sneak, avoid fights, fill bag, extract
  • PvP Hunter: Find geared player, surprise attack
  • Scout Mission: Learn map, find quest locations
  • Trade Exploit: Extract, trade augment to Lance, repeat

Meta Knowledge

23

Matchmaking

Solo players often matched vs other solos. Not guaranteed, but common. Fair fights more likely.

24

Extract While Downed

Game lets you extract when downed! Crawl into elevator/metro. Keep everything. Don't give up near extraction.

25

Scrappy is Essential

Pet rooster collects materials passively. Even while offline. Upgrade FIRST - compounds over time. Free materials = huge advantage.

26

Night Raids = Better Loot

Map condition with darkness + limited extractions. Higher risk but better loot drop rates. Blueprint chance increased. Worth it if confident.

Common Mistakes

DON'T:

  • Sell junk items (recycle instead)
  • Fight every ARC you see
  • Run in open areas
  • Ignore extraction timers
  • Forget Safe Pockets
  • Use Free Loadout for quests
  • Fight Bastion head-on
  • Stand still at extraction
  • Ignore audio cues
  • Get greedy with full bag

DO:

  • Recycle everything
  • Pick fights wisely
  • Move tactically with cover
  • Plan extractions early
  • Use Safe Pockets correctly
  • Invest in Mobility skills first
  • Flank heavy ARC
  • Stay mobile at extraction
  • Listen carefully
  • Extract when ahead
