ARC Enemy Database

Know your threats. Learn their weaknesses.

Scanner Behavior

BLUE - Patrolling

Neutral state. Safe to observe. ARC hasn't detected you yet.

YELLOW - Alerted

Heard noise or spotted at distance. Investigating. Move slowly or hide.

RED - Attacking

Locked on and engaging. Fight or flee - stealth is no longer an option.

Low Threat

Tick
Ground Ambusher
HP Very Low
XP 25

Behavior: Hides on walls and ceilings. Leaps at your face when you get close.

Weak Point: Entire body - very fragile

Drops: Minor loot, ARC Alloy

Audio Cue: Scuttling sound

Counter: Melee one-shot with Fly Swatter skill. Without it, any weapon works - just don't let them grab you.
Pop
Rolling Bomb
HP Very Low
Threat Explosive

Behavior: Rolls toward players, detonates on contact or when shot.

Weak Point: Glowing core (center)

Counter: Shoot before it reaches you. DO NOT rush - will explode in your face.
Snitch
Scout Drone
HP Low
Priority KILL FAST

Behavior: Flies high, scans area. If alerted, calls reinforcements (Wasps, Hornets).

Weak Point: 3 wiper blades (destroy all to kill)

Drops: Snitch Scanner

Counter: Kill IMMEDIATELY to prevent alarm. Destroy all 3 blades quickly before it calls backup.
Wasp
Flying Drone
HP Low
Attack Machine Gun

Behavior: Aerial machine gun fire. Relatively easy to hit.

Weak Point: Thrusters (all sides exposed)

Drops: Wasp Driver, ARC Alloy

Counter: Target propellers for quick kills. Shoot same-side propeller to make it crash, then melee finish.
Fireball
Flamethrower Unit
HP Low
Attack Flame Stream

Behavior: Patrols in groups. Stops, flames area, then rolls to next spot.

Weak Point: Entire body

Drops: Fireball Burner

Counter: Shoot while rolling (before flame attack). Avoid the flame cone.

Medium Threat

Hornet
Combat Drone
HP Medium
XP 150

Behavior: Faster than Wasp. Stun attacks that leave you vulnerable.

Weak Point: Rear thrusters (back only - harder to hit)

Drops: Hornet Driver, ARC Alloy

Danger: Stun attack leaves you vulnerable to other enemies!

Counter: Circle strafe to hit rear thrusters. Don't let it stun you in crowded areas.
Turret
Stationary Defense
HP Medium
Mobility None

Behavior: Wall-mounted. Calibrates slowly, then fires. Stationary target.

Weak Point: Base/core

Time Window: Slow to aim - exploit this!

Counter: Jump + melee from above. Or shoot the base while it calibrates.
Bison
Quadruped
HP Medium-High
Speed Fast

Behavior: Jump attacks, wave blasts. Faster than Bastion but less armored.

Weak Point: Legs/joints

Counter: Keep distance, target legs. Use cover to break its charge path.
Leaper
Ambush Unit
HP Medium
Attack Pounce

Behavior: Pounces from distance. Can close gaps quickly.

Counter: Set mines on predictable jump paths. Grenades work well. Anticipate the leap.

High Threat

Rocketeer
Aerial Artillery
⚠ #1 PLAYER KILLER
HP High
Attack Guided Rockets

Behavior: Guided rockets with AoE damage. Stays at range.

Weak Point: Thrusters, glowing core underneath

Drops: Rocketeer Driver, Heavy Gun Parts

Audio Cue: Lock-on warning sound - MOVE when you hear it!

CRITICAL: NEVER fight in open areas. Rockets have splash damage. One volley can end your raid.
Counter: ALWAYS fight from indoor cover. 1 Wolfpack Grenade = quick kill. Heavy ammo mandatory.
Bastion
Heavy Walker
⚠ NEVER FRONT ASSAULT
HP Very High
XP Massive

Behavior: Slow crab-like walker with devastating minigun.

Weak Points: Yellow kneecaps FIRST, then rear yellow cylinder

Drops: Bastion Parts, Bastion Cells (Hullcracker ammo)

CRITICAL: Frontal minigun melts shields and HP. Flanking is MANDATORY.
Counter: One player draws aggro, others flank and shoot kneecaps. Use Ferro for armor penetration.
Bombardier
Artillery Walker
⚠ KILL SPOTTERS FIRST
HP Very High
Attack Heat-Seeking Mortars

Behavior: Mortar strikes guided by Spotter drones (yellow laser).

Weak Points: Same as Bastion - knees, then rear cylinder

CRITICAL: Mortars track through air. Fight only from buildings with SOLID ROOFS.
Counter: Kill Spotters first (removes tracking). Use Bastion tactics for the walker itself.
Shredder
Close Combat Specialist
NEW
HP High
Location Stella Montis ONLY

Behavior: Rapid shotgun-like barrage at close range.

CRITICAL: Shreds shields and HP instantly if you're close. Don't round corners blindly.
Counter: Maintain distance, use cover. Stella Montis corridors are dangerous - check corners.

Boss Tier

The Queen
Harvester Guardian
Size GIGANTIC
Requires Multiple Squads

Behavior: Mortar fire + devastating laser beam. Appears during Harvester events.

Weak Point: Large core (after breaking outer armor)

Drops: Advanced Power Cells, Legendary components

Strategy: Team coordination essential. Hullcracker mandatory. Alternatively, ignore Queen and loot Harvester vaults instead - easier loot.
Matriarch
Advanced Combat Unit
NEW
Location Stella Montis
Difficulty EXTREME

Tagline: "Makes the Queen look friendly"

Behavior: Flashbangs, gas mines, summons Bastions and Rocketeers as backup.

Weak Point: Face - but ONLY when blue shield is DOWN

CRITICAL: Blue shield = invulnerable. Wait for shield drop, then unload everything.
Requirements: Hullcracker + Wolfpack Grenades + coordinated team. Solo attempts are suicide.

Lootable ARC (Non-Hostile)

Not all ARC will attack you. These units can be looted without a fight:

  • ARC Courier - Passive unit carrying materials
  • ARC Probe - Non-combat scanner, lootable components
  • ARC Husks - Dead/destroyed units. Giant spider-types have a lootable hatch on top (climb up, grab loot, jump off FAST - chassis heats up after opening)
