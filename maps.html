Maps & Locations

Five distinct battlegrounds across the Rust Belt

Dam Battlegrounds

Alcantara Power Plant
Difficulty
★ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆
Beginner

Swamps, forests, and structures centered around the Alcantara Power Plant. A major First Wave battleground, now an active zone for ARC skirmishes. The most forgiving map for new Raiders.

Best For
Learning basics, Metal Parts, Batteries, Arc Circuitry
Extraction Points
3 Elevators

Key POIs

  • ⚡ Electrical Substations - Battery farming
  • 🌿 Hydroponic Dome Complex
  • ⚙ Power Generation Complex
  • 💧 Water Treatment
  • 🔬 Testing Annex

Pro Tips

  • Great cover everywhere - use the forest to your advantage
  • Low-value loot but safe for learning
  • Avoid open water areas - limited escape routes

Buried City

Ancient Sand-Covered Ruins
Difficulty
★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
Intermediate

Long covered in sand dunes, Buried City (originally called Marano) offers rare insights into life before the Collapse. Strong winds have recently exposed portions of its ruins. Unlocks after 6 rounds.

Best For
Fabric, Cat Beds, mid-tier loot
Extraction Points
3 Metro Stations (underground)

Key POIs

  • 🏥 Hospital - High-value medical loot
  • 🏛 Town Hall
  • 🌹 Plaza Rosa - Cat Beds spawn
  • 🏠 Santa Maria Houses
  • 🛍 Galleria - Shop shelves

Pro Tips

  • Narrow streets = ambush danger. Check corners!
  • Shop shelves have great loot - don't overlook them
  • Metro extraction takes extra time - plan accordingly

Spaceport

Acerra Exodus Facility
Difficulty
★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
Intermediate

Acerra Spaceport is a majestic testament to humanity's past ambitions. A derelict launch facility with above-ground and underground sections. Unlocks after 12 rounds.

Best For
ARC Circuitry, Tech Parts, Electronics
Extraction Points
4 Elevators

Key POIs

  • ⚠ Container Storage - DANGEROUS PvP hotspot!
  • 🚀 Launch Towers
  • 🛫 Departure Building
  • 🛬 Arrival Building
  • 📡 Control Tower A6
  • ⛽ Fuel Lines

Pro Tips

  • Long sightlines favor ranged weapons - bring a DMR or Bettina
  • Container Storage is the richest but most contested area
  • Industrial areas = less cover, more danger

The Blue Gate

Mountain Pass & Tunnels
Difficulty
★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
Advanced

Open areas, small towns, tunnels, and underground complexes in higher mountain elevations. The Tubes network has only recently been expanded to reach these gates. Unlocks after 18 rounds.

Best For
High-tier loot, puzzle rewards
Extraction Points
Elevators + Raider Hatches

Key POIs

  • ⛰ Pilgrim's Peak
  • 🚧 Checkpoint - Bastion spawn!
  • 🌲 Trapper's Glade
  • 🕳 Underground tunnel network

Pro Tips

  • Puzzle rooms require specific items - come prepared
  • Hatches more common here - save your keys for emergencies
  • Underground tunnels have excellent loot but limited escape routes

Stella Montis

Underground Research Facility
NEW
Difficulty
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Expert

Carved deep into the mountainside, Stella Montis looms silently over the valley below. A massive 2-floor research facility representing remnants of humanity's lost ambitions. The most dangerous map with the best loot.

Best For
Epic loot, exclusive drops, new enemies
Extraction Points
1 Airshaft, 2 Metro Stations

Key POIs

  • ⚠ Medical Research - INTENSE PvP hotspot!
  • 🌱 Seed Vault
  • ⚙ Assembly Workshops
  • 🏢 Lobby - Long-range combat area
  • 📦 Sandbox A & B
  • 🚚 Loading Bay - Bastion patrol

Unique Enemies

Shredder Matriarch Multiple Bastions

Pro Tips

  • Close-quarters combat - bring shotguns (Vulcano ideal)
  • Dark corridors hide Shredders - check every corner
  • Airshaft extraction is unique - learn the route
  • Medical Research has best loot but highest PvP concentration

Map Conditions

Condition Effect Loot Quality Recommended For

Snowfall Reduced visibility Normal PvP avoidance, ambush builds

Loot Zone Guide

Green Zones

Low-tier loot. Safe areas with common materials. Good for beginners.

Yellow Zones

Medium-tier loot. Better rewards with moderate ARC presence.

Red Zones

High-tier loot. Best rewards but heavy ARC and PvP concentration.
