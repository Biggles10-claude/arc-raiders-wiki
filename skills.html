🏃 Mobility Tree

Speed, stamina efficiency, and traversal. The most important tree for all builds.

Tier 1 (Entry)
Nimble Climber
ESSENTIAL
Faster climb and vault speed. Required to progress.
Tier 2 (5-10 points)
Marathon Runner
ESSENTIAL
Movement costs less stamina. Critical for survival.
Youthful Lungs
ESSENTIAL
Increased maximum stamina pool.
Proper Breathing
IMPORTANT
Faster stamina regeneration.
Tier 3 (10-20 points)
Effortless Roll
ESSENTIAL
Dodge rolls cost less stamina. Key for PvP.
Calming Stroll
IMPORTANT
Stamina regenerates while walking.
Carry the Momentum
SITUATIONAL
Speed boost after dodging.
Tier 4 (20-30 points)
Vault on Vaults
IMPORTANT
Zero stamina cost for vaulting.
Light Feet
ESSENTIAL
Faster sprint speed.
Nimble Recovery
SITUATIONAL
Faster stamina recovery after depletion.

💪 Conditioning Tree

Toughness, recovery, and weight management. Essential for combat builds.

Tier 1 (Entry)
Used to the Weight
ESSENTIAL
Less shield speed penalty. Required to progress.
Tier 2
Gentle Pressure
IMPORTANT
Quieter breaching. Great for stealth.
Proficient Pryer
IMPORTANT
Faster breaching speed.
Heavier Hitter
SITUATIONAL
Increased melee damage.
Tier 3 (15 points)
Survivor's Stamina
ESSENTIAL
Faster stamina regen when hurt.
Fight or Flight
IMPORTANT
Auto stamina regen at low HP.
Unburdened Roll
SITUATIONAL
Free dodge when shield breaks.
Tier 4 & Advanced (36+ points)
Downed but Determined
IMPORTANT
Longer bleed-out timer. Team play essential.
Back on Your Feet
SITUATIONAL
Health regen at critical HP.
Fly Swatter
IMPORTANT
One-hit melee on Ticks and Wasps.

🎒 Survival Tree

Scavenging, stealth, and field crafting. Late-game power spikes.

Tier 1 (Entry)
Agile Croucher
IMPORTANT
Faster crouch movement. Required to progress.
Tier 2
Looter's Instincts
ESSENTIAL
Faster item identification.
Silent Scavenger
ESSENTIAL
Quieter looting. Huge for stealth.
Broad Shoulders
IMPORTANT
Increased carry capacity.
Tier 3
Just Another Plant
IMPORTANT
Harder for ARC to spot when crouching.
Revitalizing Squat
SITUATIONAL
Stamina regen while crouching.
Tier 4 (36 points) - GAME CHANGERS
In-Round Crafting
CRITICAL
Craft bandages, shield cells, and grenades during raids. Changes everything.
Security Breach
ESSENTIAL
Access Security Lockers for high-tier loot.
Traveling Tinkerer
IMPORTANT
Expanded field crafting options.

Recommended Builds

Solo Survivor
Self-Sufficiency
  • Max Mobility (all essential skills)
  • Gentle Pressure + Silent Scavenger (stealth)
  • In-Round Crafting (self-heal/sustain)
  • Revitalizing Squat + Calming Stroll

Designed for running alone. Sustainability, stealth, and self-reliance.

Team Support
Group Utility
  • Mobility essentials
  • Downed but Determined + Turtle Crawl
  • Traveling Tinkerer (craft for team)
  • Broad Shoulders maxed (carry team loot)

Built to keep your squad alive and well-supplied.

PvP Hunter
Combat Dominance
  • Max Mobility + Combat Conditioning
  • Fight or Flight + Survivor's Stamina
  • Skip crafting, focus on combat sustainability
  • Light Feet for aggressive positioning

Built to find and eliminate other Raiders.

Speed Looter
Maximum Efficiency
  • All Mobility skills
  • All Survival looting skills
  • Broad Shoulders maxed
  • Security Breach for extra loot

In fast, loot everything, out faster. Maximum extraction value.
