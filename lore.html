The Lore of ARC Raiders

The story of humanity's last stand

Timeline of Catastrophe

The First Wave: Climate Collapse

Earth ravaged by climate catastrophe. Civilization teeters on the brink. Desperate attempts at salvation lead to the construction of Exodus shuttles at Spaceport Acerra. Hope for space colonies emerges.

The Second Wave: ARC Arrival

Mysterious machines appear from the sky. Unknown origin, unknown purpose. Designated "ARC" (Artificial Robotic Construct). Systematic destruction of humanity begins. The surface becomes the "Rust Belt." Humanity is driven underground.

The Underground Era

Survivors build Speranza in post-apocalyptic Italy, beneath the District of Toledo. Leaders emerge: Celeste, Shani, and others. A society of "Raiders" forms - risk-takers who venture topside for supplies.

Present Day

Raids for resources are essential for survival. The ARC threat is ever-present. Factions hold different visions for the future. The mystery of ARC's origin remains unsolved. Evacuation plans are being prepared. The fight continues...

The ARC Mystery

What We Know

  • Mechanical entities of immense power
  • Variety of units (ground, air, boss-class)
  • Seem to "rain from the sky"
  • Methodical, intelligent behavior
  • Origins completely unknown

What We Don't Know

  • Who created them?
  • Why did they appear?
  • What is their purpose?
  • Can they be stopped?
  • Is there a controller?

Speranza: The Last City

Location

Deep underground beneath Italy's ruins, in the District of Toledo. Multiple neighborhoods have formed, with a growing population of survivors.

Culture

  • NASA-Punk aesthetic: Cowboy dusters meet astronaut helmets

The Rust Belt

The surface world. Post-post-apocalyptic Italy, overgrown and reclaimed by nature. Deadly beautiful.

Regions

  • Spaceport: Exodus shuttle facility
  • Blue Gate: Mountain pass
  • Stella Montis: Research facility

Aesthetic

  • Colorful despite the danger
