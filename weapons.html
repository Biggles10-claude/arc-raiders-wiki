Weapon Modifications

Barrel Mods

Suppressors reduce sound but decrease damage. Standard barrels maintain damage output. Choose based on playstyle.

Sight Mods

Red dots for close-medium range. Scopes for long range. Iron sights work fine for most encounters.

Grip Mods

Vertical Grip III is the meta choice. Significantly reduces recoil for better sustained fire accuracy.

Extended Mags

More bullets before reload. Essential for PvP fights where reload timing can be fatal.
