Core Concepts

⌂

Speranza

Your underground home base. A thriving community beneath the ruins of Italy where you craft gear, accept quests from traders, and prepare for the dangers above.

◎

The Rust Belt

The dangerous surface world. Five distinct maps filled with loot, hostile ARC machines, and other Raiders. Post-apocalyptic Italy reclaimed by nature.

⚔

PvPvE Combat

Fight ARC machines AND other players. Every raid is a dynamic mix of environmental threats, AI enemies, and human opponents with their own agendas.

↑

Extraction

Keep what you loot - but only if you extract alive. Die and you lose everything not in your Safe Pocket. The stakes are always real.

What Makes It Different

▶ Third-Person Perspective

Unlike most extraction shooters, ARC Raiders uses a third-person camera. This changes everything - you can see around corners, check your six, and appreciate the incredible world Embark built. It also makes parkour and traversal feel more fluid and rewarding.

▶ ARC Machines as Genuine Threats

The AI enemies aren't just fodder - they're genuinely dangerous. A Rocketeer will ruin your day. A Bastion requires strategy. The Queen needs multiple squads. PvE matters as much as PvP, and smart Raiders know when to avoid fights entirely.

▶ Free Loadout System

Completely broke? Take a Free Loadout - basic gear at zero cost. No Safe Pockets (you lose everything if you die), but zero investment means zero risk. Perfect for learning, scouting, or gearing up from nothing.

▶ NASA-Punk Aesthetic

The visual style is unique: cowboy dusters meet astronaut helmets. Post-apocalyptic Italy with 70s space age vibes. It's called "NASA-Punk" - bold, colorful, hopeful despite the danger. Not grimdark, but not naive either.

▶ Speranza as a Living Hub

Your home base isn't just menus - it's a place. Walk through neighborhoods, chat with traders, see other Raiders preparing for drops. Speranza has personality: Apollo runs a kids' football team, Lance does midnight karaoke, the library grows with community efforts.
